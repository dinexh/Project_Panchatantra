Our Partners!

We are grateful for the support and collaboration of various partners in our Panchatantra project. Together with the Andhra Pradesh Forest Department, local government bodies, NGOs, educational institutions, corporate partners, community groups, research institutions, and international agencies, we are working towards the development of urban and rural forests. Their contributions in terms of resources, expertise, funding, and community engagement are instrumental in our efforts to protect and preserve the environment for future generations.

Join Us!

Join us in Panchatantra, a transformative project aimed at developing urban and rural forests to protect our planet. Together, we can make a difference by planting trees, establishing water bodies, and improving groundwater levels. Let's create a greener, more sustainable future for generations to come. Join the movement today!.
